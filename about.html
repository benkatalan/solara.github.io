About Surfly.io

Welcome to Surfly.io, your ultimate companion for catching the perfect wave! We're passionate surfers who understand the importance of accurate, real-time surf forecasts.

Our Mission

At Surfly.io, we're on a mission to provide surfers worldwide with the most accurate and up-to-date surf forecasts. Whether you're a beginner or a pro, we've got you covered with detailed information on wave heights, wind conditions, and water temperatures.

Data That Makes a Difference

We source our data from world-renowned meteorological services and oceanographic institutes. Our primary data provider is World Weather Online, known for its high-quality marine weather data. We complement this with local reports and satellite imagery to ensure you get the most accurate forecast possible.

Features

  • Real-time surf forecasts for beaches worldwide
  • Detailed information on wave height, wind speed and direction, and water temperature
  • 7-day forecast to help you plan your surf sessions
  • User-friendly interface designed for quick and easy access to information
  • Customizable alerts for your favorite surf spots

Join the Surfly.io Community

Surfly.io is more than just an app - it's a community of surf enthusiasts. Join us in our quest for the perfect wave, share your experiences, and connect with fellow surfers around the globe.

Stay tuned for exciting new features coming soon, including user-generated surf reports, a social platform for surfers, and integration with surf cam feeds!
